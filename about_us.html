We are a mission-driven team based out of India

Our company was started with the mission of making product development faster and more efficient. Coming from a product development background ourselves, we have incorporated our learnings from years of experience building both software and non-software products into making KLARK. We use KLARK to manage our own product development lifecycles and we have noticed it saves our developer teams 100s of hours switching between application windows, going through data and interpreting it.

Our company culture is built around 3 fundamental beliefs that we set for ourselves before embarking on this long journey:

  • 100% Customer Satisfaction
  • Platform Customizability
  • Data-driven Decisions
